How Blog Posts Work
  1. Write a draft — Your drafts are internal and won't be visible to anyone except you and staff.
  2. Use Markdown — Posts are written in Markdown format, which lets you add formatting like bold, italic, headings, and lists.
  3. Request review — Once your draft is ready, let any administrator know and they will review it for publication.
  4. After publication — Once your post is published, editing or deleting it on the website is no longer possible. Contact an administrator if you need changes.
