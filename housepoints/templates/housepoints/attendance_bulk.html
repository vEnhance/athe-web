How to Use
  1. Select a class from the dropdown
  2. Click "Load Students" to see enrolled students
  3. All students are checked by default
  4. Uncheck any students who were absent
  5. Choose 5 points (default) or 3 points
  6. Click "Award Attendance Points"
Notes
  • Only shows classes from active semesters
  • Students must have a house assigned
  • Points are auto-assigned to student's house
  • Award type is automatically "Class Attendance"
