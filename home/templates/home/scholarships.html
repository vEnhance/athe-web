Scholarships

In the 2025-2026 year, we are running a scholarship program for female and nonbinary students who wish to attend in-person contests, but cannot afford to. We are sponsoring several trips, each of up to $300 value, to an in-person math tournament of choice, for female or nonbinary students, whether or not they participate on Athemath teams.

The $300 may cover travel costs, food costs, hotel costs, transportation costs, and any other costs related to the trip. The money will be provided as a reimbursement check after we receive receipts for any costs related to the trip.

This opportunity is only available to students who would otherwise not be able to attend these events. It is not free money for families who would regularly be able to afford this trip.

Currently, we are only opening up this opportunity to Virtual Program participants and alumni. We may also open up this opportunity to non-alumni at a later time.
